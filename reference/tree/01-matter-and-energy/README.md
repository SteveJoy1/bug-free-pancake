# 01 — Matter and Energy

Propaedia Part 1. The physical universe: particles, forces, fields, matter, energy, space, time.

## Divisions (modernized)

Propaedia's 1974 divisions under this Part have been partially superseded. This skeleton uses **PhySH** (Physics Subject Headings, AIP/APS, 2016) concepts as Divisions and Wikidata as the leaf-identifier source.

Planned files:

- `classical-mechanics.yaml` — motion, forces, energy, momentum at human scales. **[populated as worked example]**
- `electromagnetism.yaml` — charges, fields, waves, radiation.
- `thermodynamics-and-statistical-physics.yaml` — heat, entropy, statistical mechanics.
- `quantum-mechanics.yaml` — atoms, particles, wavefunctions.
- `relativity.yaml` — special, general, spacetime.
- `atomic-and-nuclear-physics.yaml` — atomic structure, nuclei, radiation.
- `particle-physics.yaml` — standard model and beyond.
- `condensed-matter.yaml` — solids, liquids, emergent phenomena.
- `cosmology-and-astrophysics.yaml` — universe at large scales.

## Notes

- Wikidata IDs in leaf files should be verified against wikidata.org before being relied on.
- A leaf belongs here if it is a concept, law, phenomenon, or quantity in physics — not a tool, person, or historical event.
