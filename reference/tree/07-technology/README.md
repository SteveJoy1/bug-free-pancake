# 07 — Technology

Propaedia Part 7. Applied knowledge: engineering, computing, medicine, agriculture, energy, transport, materials.

## Divisions

Propaedia's 1974 divisions here are heavily pre-digital. Modern grafts:

- **Computing** uses [ACM CCS](https://dl.acm.org/ccs) (2012).
- **Medical technology** will use [MeSH](https://www.nlm.nih.gov/mesh/).
- **Engineering** broadly uses IEEE subject taxonomy as a reference where relevant.
- **Patent classification** ([IPC](https://www.wipo.int/classifications/ipc/)) provides a sanity-check for the applied-technology branches.

## Notes

- A leaf belongs here if it's an applied technique, process, device, or artifact — not a scientific law (those go in Part 01 Matter & Energy or Part 03 Life on Earth) and not a social structure (Part 05).
- When computing concepts have a mathematical basis (e.g., Big-O, graph theory), the canonical home is `computing/` but they should `see_also` their mathematical parent in Part 10 when that Part is populated.
